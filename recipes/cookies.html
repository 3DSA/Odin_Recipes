Cookie Recipe

Description

Steps on how to make delicious eggless cookies!

Ingredients

  • all purpose flour
  • sugar
  • butter
  • milk
  • chocolate chips
  • vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Leave out butter to let it get to room temperature
  2. Put butter and sugar in a bowl and mix until smooth
  3. Add flour and vanilla extract to mix, and continue to mix
  4. Add milk to bowl and continue mixing
  5. Once smooth, take dough and roll them into balls, put chocolate chips on top of balls
  6. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  7. Once oven is 350 F, place the cookie dough into oven
  8. After 10 minutes, remove the cookies from the oven and enjoy!
